What is this page?

This website is  a part of the campaign #ReOpenPU and  this web page helps you to easily compose the email template which is drafted by the Students' Council in  One Click.

Why this campaign?

"Education is a right, not a privilege. Don't let rising tuition fees deny students access to quality education."
Our university fee hike campaign aims to raise awareness and advocate for affordable education. The increasing cost of tuition fees is making it harder for students to access higher education, limiting their potential and future opportunities.
We believe that education should be accessible to all, regardless of their financial background. We urge the university administration to re-evaluate the current fee structure and come up with solutions that prioritize students' financial well-being while maintaining the quality of education.
Join us in this campaign and make your voice heard. Let's work together towards a more inclusive and equitable education system.
